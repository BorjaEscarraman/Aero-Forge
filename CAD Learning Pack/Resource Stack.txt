Always ensure you know...

Function: What does this part do?
Interfaces: What does it touch?
Datums: What locates it?
Loads / pressure: What forces or fluid pressure matter?
Manufacturing: How would it be made?
Assembly: Can it be installed and serviced?
Drawing: Can someone else manufacture it from the drawing?
Revision: Can I change key dimensions without breaking the model?
Verification: Did I check mass, interference, and section views?
GitHub: Did I document what changed and what I learned?

Metric Ruler dimensions.
Hook #1 (super simple):
“Big numbers = centimeters. Tiny ticks = millimeters. 10 tiny ticks per big number.”

Hook #2 (counting mantra):
“Find the last big number… then count the tiny ticks.”
(Last cm, then +mm)

Hook #3 (visual):
“Long = 1 cm, medium = 5 mm, short = 1 mm.”

If you want one single line to tattoo into your brain:
“Big = cm, small = mm, 10 small = 1 big.”
